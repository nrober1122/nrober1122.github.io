The PhD thesis proposal is an important step towards earning a PhD.
It demonstrates your technical readiness and presents a concrete plan to your committee that shows you have what it takes to conduct PhD research and finish within a specified timeline.
This CommKit article serves as a guide towards creating and defending a convincing PhD proposal to help launch your PhD career.
